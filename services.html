Our responsibilities

At Higley Manor HOA, we strive to provide our residents with the best quality of life possible. That's why we offer a range of services to meet your needs and enhance your living experience. Here are some of the services we offer:

  • Lawn Care: We take care of mowing, trimming, and fertilizing your lawn on a regular basis. You don't have to worry about maintaining your yard or hiring a contractor.
  • Snow Removal: We clear the snow from your driveway, sidewalk, and porch during the winter months. You can enjoy the snow without the hassle of shoveling or salting.
  • Trash Collection: We collect your trash and recycling every week. You just have to place your bins at the curb on the designated day.
  • Pest Control: We provide pest control services to prevent and eliminate any unwanted insects or rodents from your home. You can rest assured that your home is pest-free and safe.
  • Maintenance and Repairs: We handle any maintenance and repairs that may arise in your home or common areas. Whether it's a leaky faucet, a broken window, or a faulty appliance, we have you covered.
  • Security: We provide security services to ensure the safety and peace of mind of our residents. We have a gated entrance, a security guard on duty, and a surveillance system in place.
  • Landscaping: We beautify and maintain the common areas of our community. We plant flowers, trees, and shrubs, and we water and weed them regularly. We also take care of the sidewalks, fences, and signs.
  • Recreation: We offer a variety of recreational facilities and activities for our residents. We have a swimming pool, a tennis court, a playground, and a clubhouse. We also organize social events, such as potlucks, bingo nights, and movie nights.

If you have any questions or requests regarding our services, please contact us at (555) 555-5555 or email us at higleymanorhoa@gmail.com. We are always happy to assist you.
